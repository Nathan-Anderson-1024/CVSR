About Us

Central Valley Stone Restoration, Inc. opened its doors in 2008. Our family run business represent three generations of quality experience in the tile and stone fabrication business. This experience allows us to perform both commercial and residential projects of all sizes. No job is too large or small. We pride ourselves in employing experienced individuals and utilizing the highest quality materials to ensure that our customers receive the level of quality and value they deserve.
Most of our customer base is repeat business because we treat people the way we want to be treated. Quality workmanship and one of the best warranties in the industry is why our customers keep coming back. Please contact us to set up a consultation for your tile or stone needs. All estimates are free of charge.
